# Knowledge Base: Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) - USA

## The Privacy Rule
* **PHI Definition:** Any information about health status, provision of health care, or payment that can be linked to an individual.
* **Minimum Necessary Standard:** Disclosures must be limited to the minimum amount of information necessary to achieve the purpose.

## The Security Rule (Technical Safeguards)
* **Access Control:** Unique user IDs, emergency access procedures, and automatic log-offs.
* **Audit Controls:** Hardware, software, and/or procedural mechanisms that record and examine activity in information systems.
* **Integrity:** Protection of PHI from improper alteration or destruction.
* **Transmission Security:** Encryption of PHI during transit (e.g., TLS 1.2+).

## Administrative Requirements
* **BAA (Business Associate Agreement):** Must be signed with any third party handling PHI (e.g., AWS, Google Cloud).
