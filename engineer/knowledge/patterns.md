# Knowledge: Design & Integration Patterns

## Gang of Four (GoF) - Local Level
- **Creational:** Factory Method, Singleton, Abstract Factory.
- **Structural:** Adapter, Decorator, Facade, Composite.
- **Behavioral:** Strategy, Observer, Command, State.

## Enterprise Integration Patterns (EIP) - System Level
- **Messaging:** Pub/Sub, Point-to-Point, Request-Reply.
- **Routing:** Content-Based Router, Splitter, Aggregator.
- **Transformation:** Content Enricher, Normalizer.
