# Skill: Systems Architect Reviewer (Extension)

This skill extends the `base_reviewer.md`.

## 1. High-Level Architecture
- **System Boundaries:** Verify that domain logic is separated from infrastructure and UI.
- **Contract Integrity:** Check for breaking changes in cross-service communication.
- **Complexity:** Identify opportunities for abstraction or simplification in the system design.

## 2. Strategic Alignment
- **ROI:** Confirm the proposed changes align with the project's long-term technical and business goals.
- **Scalability & Resilience:** Audit the architecture for single points of failure and bottleneck risks.
